Designed for private daily context

No Duck Code account. No sale of personal data.

VeinTrack stores app data on your device unless you choose to export, email, save, back up, or share it elsewhere. Apple Health and Motion data are used only after permission is granted.

Local first Your records stay on device by default.
Permission based Health, Motion, notifications, and photos remain under iOS controls.
User controlled sharing Reports and exports leave the app only when you send or save them.

Important notes

Useful, private, and intentionally low pressure.

Not medical advice VeinTrack supports personal tracking and preparation. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace a clinician.
Free app and payment safety VeinTrack is free to use. The only legitimate payments are optional in-app support purchases through Apple. If anyone asks you to pay outside the app or App Store flow, treat it as a scam and report it.
Support is optional Feedback, sharing with someone who may benefit, and optional StoreKit support are all appreciated. None unlock features.

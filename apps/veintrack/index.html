Designed for private daily context

100% local unless you choose to share.

VeinTrack keeps your app data on your device. It does not share your data with anyone or send it to the web unless you choose to export, email, save, back up, or share it.

Local by default Your records stay inside the app unless you choose otherwise.
Permission based Health, Motion, notifications, and photos remain under iOS controls.
User controlled sharing Reports and exports leave the app only when you send or save them.

Activity tracker and widget

A visible nudge when a session is running.

Coached activity mode can keep rest actions and session status close at hand. The widget can show daily steps and distance, start a coached session, log rest, or open the app.

Passive detection stays private and local, but iOS may delay background updates. Coached sessions are more timely because you intentionally start them.

What you can do

Daily context, reminders, history, and reports without turning the app into pressure.

VeinTrack is built around small, private records: what happened today, what you want to remember later, and what you may choose to export.

Daily check-ins

Use the dashboard and Quick View to capture weight, activity, sleep, leg elevation, symptoms, and photos from one calm daily flow.

Activity, rest, and travel context

Keep walking context, rest prompts, Flight Mode records, and care events visible together, with iOS background limits clearly framed.

Symptoms, photos, and care records

Keep pain, swelling, leg elevation, notes, comparison photos, consultation context, and compression details close to the date they belong to.

Calendar, stats, and reports

Use calendar markers, insight cards, charts, and summaries to prepare reports or exports only when you choose to share them.

Settings, reminders, and support

Manage feedback, sharing, reminders, preferences, tracked limbs, compression wear, and optional support without feature locks.

Privacy

Local by default, shared only by choice.

VeinTrack keeps app data on your device. It does not share your data with anyone or send it to the web unless you choose to export, email, save, back up, or share it.

Read the full privacy policy

No Duck Code account There is no Duck Code backend account for VeinTrack data.
Permission based Health, Motion, notifications, and photos remain under iOS controls.
User controlled exports Reports, backups, files, and photos leave the app only when you send or save them.

VeinTrack Beta

Private vein-health tracking for everyday context.

VeinTrack helps people track vein-health-related daily context on device, including symptoms, leg elevation, compression use, activity and rest sessions, weight, sleep, travel context, consultations, photos, and reports.

VeinTrack supports personal tracking, reminders, and report preparation. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice.

Private by design

Built around on-device tracking.

VeinTrack does not require a Duck Code account. Personal tracking data is intended to stay on your iPhone unless you choose to export, share, back up, email, or attach it elsewhere.

  • No Duck Code backend account.
  • No sale of personal data.
  • Apple Health and Motion access only after permission is granted.

Quick View

Save the essentials on busy days.

Quick View lets you save essential daily information quickly, then keeps those entries as normal app events so they can appear in stats, insights, reports, exports, and calendar summaries.

Activity and rest coaching

Comfort-focused prompts with clear limits.

VeinTrack can use on-device activity context to help identify longer walking or running sessions and remind you to rest. Passive reminders may be delayed by iOS background scheduling. Coached activity sessions are more timely because you intentionally start or accept an active session.

The app frames reminders as comfort and rest prompts, not medical instructions.

Daily events and photos

Keep useful context together.

Record symptoms, leg elevation, compression use, compression prescription notes, sleep, weight, travel and Flight Mode records, consultations, appointment notes, and photos for personal comparison.

Photos and files remain part of local app data unless you choose to export, share, email, back up, or save them elsewhere.

Calendar and history

Browse records by day instead of memory.

Calendar summaries and event history help turn small daily entries into a more navigable timeline. You can review what happened, when it happened, and which notes or attachments belong with each record.

Stats, insights, and reports

Turn tracked context into shareable summaries.

VeinTrack can generate reports from your tracked data. Reports can include summaries, charts, insights, photo comparisons, consultation records, activity and rest context, and appendices with raw data.

You decide when to export or share them.

Widgets and Live Activities

Keep sessions visible when you choose.

VeinTrack may use notifications, widgets, and Live Activities to show reminders and activity or rest session status. These features are controlled by iOS permissions and user settings.

Export and share

Reports, CSV exports, and backups stay under your control.

VeinTrack supports PDF reports, HTML email summaries, CSV export, and backups. Generated reports, files, and backups are only shared when you choose to export, email, save, or send them elsewhere.

Privacy

No backend account, no sale of user data.

Depending on configuration, VeinTrack may handle user-entered health context, pain and swelling scores, rest records, compression details, activity session records, Apple Health sleep and weight data when permission is granted, Motion activity data when permission is granted, user-attached photos, consultation details, reports, exports, backups, and optional StoreKit support purchase status.

About Privacy Licensing Support

Free app and payment safety VeinTrack is free to use. The only legitimate payments are optional in-app support purchases through Apple. If anyone asks you to pay outside the app or App Store flow, treat it as a scam and report it.

Optional support

Optional financial support may be available inside the app through Apple StoreKit. It is always optional, does not unlock features, and does not change app behavior.

Privacy Policy

VeinTrack is designed to work without a Duck Code backend account. App data stays on your device unless you choose to export, email, save, back up, or share it.

About Privacy Licensing Support

Effective date: July 3, 2026.

Data the app may handle

Depending on how you use VeinTrack, the app may handle health context you enter, notes, pain and swelling scores, leg elevation and rest records, compression details, activity sessions, travel and Flight Mode records, consultations, attachments, photos, reports, exports, backups, and optional support purchase status from Apple StoreKit.

With permission, VeinTrack may also use Apple Health sleep and weight data, and Motion or activity context, to support reminders, summaries, charts, and reports.

Local-first design

Duck Code does not require a VeinTrack account, does not run a VeinTrack backend for personal health data, and does not sell VeinTrack user data. Apple Health, Motion, notifications, photos, and related permissions can be managed through iOS.

Sharing, exports, and backups

Photos, files, reports, PDFs, HTML summaries, CSV files, and backups leave the app only when you choose to export, email, save, back up, or share them. After that, they are handled by the destination you choose.

Optional support and payment safety

VeinTrack is free to use. The only legitimate payments are optional in-app support purchases through Apple's App Store system. They never unlock features. If anyone asks you to pay outside the app or App Store flow, treat it as a scam and report it.

Feedback email

If you send feedback, your email is handled by your chosen mail provider and received at duck_code_contact@proton.me. Please avoid sending sensitive information unless you choose to include it.

Deleting data

Deleting the app deletes local app data from the device, except for anything you previously exported, shared, backed up, emailed, saved to Files, or stored in another service.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to duck_code_contact@proton.me.
